About

Khajiit has wares, if you have coin...

Khajiit searches G2A and Steam, then compares the prices and returns an embedded message with links to both and a price comparison. Use '/game <name of game>' to search.

Currently Khajiit only responds with Global & North American results in USD $.

Command

/game <name of game>

Response Links

  • Main result.
  • Search results.
  • G2A purchase link & price
  • Steam purchase link & price (if available)
  • Discount % between the two
